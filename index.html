Shahryar Khorasani / Neuroscientist / Surrealist

ABOUT ME

Hello. I'm Shahryar Khorasani. I was born in 1993 in Iran and currently I live in Berlin where I study Medical Neuroscience.

My art is mostly surrealistic with some abstract mixtures once in a while. I draw portraits, figures and ocassionally landscapes. I mostly use pencil, pen, ink and sometimes soft pastels and watercolor. I also like mixed media and I'm doing more of that lately.

I'm inspired by emotions, social issues, nature, geometric algebra and neurobiology. I often put codes and patterns for my audience to decipher the thoughts behind a drawing. However, I believe any interpretation is true and I love to hear what my viewers think of my works.

Beside art, I work on computer vision for neuroimaging. Right now, I'm developing neural networks to extract quantitative features from MRI scans and planning to try out tattoo art.
