Shahryar Khorasani is an amateur artist based in Berlin. His style lingers in a dark surrealistic space, where portraits deform into severed dimensions and figures go through cosmic transformations. The amalgam of abstraction and distinction makes each artwork appear like a window to a murky dream. Using pencil and ink as his mediums allows Shahryar to render the fine details that give life to his surrealistic drawings. Some of his artworks display impressions from neuroscience and biology.
