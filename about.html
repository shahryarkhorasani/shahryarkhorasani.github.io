Khorasani


“a window to a murky dream” - S.K.

Shahryar Khorasani is a surrealist and scientist based in Berlin. He uses pencil and ink as his mediums. His works depict an unearthly world where bodies deform and shatter into severed dimensions and physical perceptions are rendered void. The eyes linger in the layers of detail that when put together, tell a different story for each person. Many of his works display impressions from neuroscience. Shahryar has a degree in neuroscience and is currently studying his PhD in machine learning and digital health.
